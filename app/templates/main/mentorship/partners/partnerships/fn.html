About

We are dedicated to answering all furlough-related questions. In response to the recent influx of furloughed workers, our team rushed to create this site to provide info, support, and resources for the talented, hard-working people who find themselves on temporary furlough.

Our team is comprised of really smart contributors, all of whom have also had their careers significantly impacted by the effects of the recent epidemic.

We're here to help our community get through these times together. We're also committed to building a community. Let's get together and support each other now, as we prepare for the resurgence ahead!

Meet The Founders

Sean W. Hand

Life is crazy right now in 2020. I got one too many calls from incredibly smart, talented people saying "I've been furloughed". I decided I wanted to do something about it, and here we are! By day, I am a business development director at a global professional services firm. By night, I also have a website/podcast called Awkward Networker where I help people build their personal/professional networks. Not much handshaking happening right now, so I'm building this out instead. Stay positive. Do good.
